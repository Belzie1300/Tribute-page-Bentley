Bentley W. Hergstrom

The dog who loves all and by all

Here's a timeline of Bentley W. Hergstrom's life:

  • 2008 - Born in Melbourne, an English Pointer
  • 2011 - Leaves his first family home in the suburbia due to being a destructive puppy and found new home on acreage. There, he became besties with Lilman the Bloodhound and Tilly the Stagehound
  • 2012 - The Trio would stay together, take naps together, egg each other on and get into mischief but there was never any quarrels between them. Bentley was found to be a picky eater, knows what he likes and likes to keep clean.
  • 2013 - Enjoying life to the fullest, he would have the whole acreage to explore multiple times a day. Being surrounded but lots of bird life and trees, he will stalk them and have his famous trait of the breed 'pointing' staring quietly at his preys but never really catch anything
  • 2015 - He was caught in action of climbing the wired fence to our neighbour. Although he loves his 5 acres territory, to him, it was not enough and was found wondering in the neighboring 5 acres multiple times, it became his favourite pastime
  • 2016 - Athena the rescued bloodhound joined the family, who unfortunately came from a traumatic upbringing. Although she did not get along with Lilman and Tilly out of jealousy but strangely found comfort being around Bentley
  • 2017 - Bentley will always get along with other dogs of different sizes who would come to stay for a period
  • 2019 - With the family being hectic with 4 big dogs, Bentley being a fence climber, he evidently spends most of his time with the neighbour of an older couple who welcomes Bentley with love. So much so, the neighbour took him in as their own, where he found his forever home and was given the middle name "Windsor" to add to his gentlemen's demeanor
  • 2020 - Bentley would always come back over for occassional visits for cuddles, sometimes steal chicken eggs from us and would gladly trott back to his "other" loving family. As he got a bit older and not as agile as he once were, they installed a green push-pull gate with a ramp for Bentley to easily access both properties
  • 2021 - With Lilman and Tilly passed, Eva the bloodhound puppy was welcomed to the family and Bentley would come over to greet the new pup and deal with her boisterous behaviour
  • 2023 - To this day, Bentley is still going strong but does not visit as often due to arthritis in his back legs. However, he would visit the fence line for pats
